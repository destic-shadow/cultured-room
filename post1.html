General Chat Rules

1. Encourage Engagement

Upvote comments to promote interaction and activity within our community.

2. Light-Hearted Humor

Trolling is allowed as long as it's harmless and in good taste.

3. Respect Boundaries

Avoid recruiting or advertising other chatrooms.

4. Keep It Respectful

Maintain a positive tone; avoid trash-talking.

5. Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to the community's interests.

6. No Spam

Avoid excessive messaging or repetitive content.

7. Be Mindful

Consider others when sharing content or expressing opinions.
